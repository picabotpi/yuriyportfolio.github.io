Hello everybody, I am

Yuriy Mikhailidi


My story begins in Kazakhstan. I lived most of my life in the city of Almaty. I moved to United States six years ago, and now I can proudly call myself a citizen of this country. I chose the University of Colorado Boulder for all the opportunities it has to offer to its students. I am currently majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Technology Arts and Media.

My passion is Computer Science; however, I have always been fascinated with math as well as science. I was influenced by my father to become a computer scientist. We always had a computer in the household; and, I loved to watch my dad work, even though I never understood what he was doing. I can proudly say that he is my hero and I strive to be like him. I am proud that in two years while being at CU Boulder, I learned how to build programs, webpages, and learned multiple computer languages and development environments. I believe in diversifying myself in variates of fields such as: digital media, network security, transaction systems, and system architecture. I love what I am studying, my family and the University of Colorado Boulder.
